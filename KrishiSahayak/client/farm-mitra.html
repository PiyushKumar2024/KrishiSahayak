Analyzing your query... Yellowing leaves in paddy in the Prayagraj region during September can be caused by a few issues. Let me help you diagnose.

This is likely either a Nitrogen deficiency or a pest like the Yellow Stem Borer.

  • To check for Stem Borer: Go to a yellowing plant and pull the central leaf (the 'tiller'). If it comes out easily, it is a sign of 'dead heart' caused by the borer.
  • If it's Nitrogen deficiency: A top dressing of Urea fertilizer may be needed.
